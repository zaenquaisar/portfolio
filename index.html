About Me

I'm a Senior Mechanical Engineering student at Northeastern University completing a Plus One Masters in Energy Systems, expected to graduate Fall 2026. My passion lies in renewable energy deployment and creating sustainable engineering solutions that can be adapted to diverse markets and geographies.

With hands-on experience in solar tracking systems, thermal analysis, and power management, I'm focused on bridging the gap between innovative technology and practical energy applications. My long-term goal is to own a renewable energy deployment company that serves residential, commercial, and utility-scale projects with technology solutions tailored to specific regional needs.

Currently, I'm working on advanced capstone projects involving dual-axis solar trackers and solar pyrolysis systems, combining mechanical design, electronics, and software integration. I'm also seeking international experience post-graduation to gain diverse perspectives in renewable energy deployment.
